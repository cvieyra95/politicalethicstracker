The battle for the Orange County district turned harshly personal in the closing weeks of the campaign.

Democrat Derek Tran narrowly defeated Republican incumbent Michelle Steel in California's 45th congressional district, flipping the seat after a prolonged vote count and a campaign marked by personal attacks. Tran, the son of Vietnamese refugees, leveraged his background to appeal to the district's significant Asian American population, while Steel criticized his legal career; the race became the most expensive House contest of 2024, with over $46 million spent.
